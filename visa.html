China Travel Visa

Chinese travel visas are required for all U.S. Citizens and may be required for many foreign nationals prior to leaving the United States. We can guide you through the Chinese visa application process and help you obtain your Chinese travel visa in time for your trip!

Validity: 10 years
4 Business Days
Embassy fee: $140
Service fee: $50

Needed Documents:

With at least 6 months of remaining validity and at least 2 consecutive blank visa pages.

Include a passport style photo, with a white background, taken within the last 6 months. Photos with bare shoulders are not accepted.

Copy of round trip tickets or detailed itinerary for the duration of the trip (Applicant’s name must be on itinerary).

Copy of hotel reservations (Applicant’s name must be on Hotel Reservation).

If your travel is being organized by a tour group in China, an official signed and stamped Invitation Letter for Tourist issued by Duly Authorized Tourism Unit in China can be used in place of the hotel reservation. Invitation must not be issued by a US travel agency.
